How it works
Each week you'll get an email with a short lesson and an action plan.

You can reply to the email with any questions or to request feedback. You can schedule a meeting at any time.

Some things you'll learn:
It's all about the customer.
Your brand isn't an extension of you. It's a mirror of your customers--your favorite customers. You know, the good ones, the funny ones, the ones that make you remember why you got into this business in the first place. You want more of those. And this is how you'll get them. 
Positioning
You may not be familiar with this one. You won't find it plastered on a billboard or website. Positioning is an internal exercise that brings focus to every part of your business. Remember that favorite customer? Positioning is like a favorite customer magnet. Get it right and you'll love going to work everyday. 
Personality and Voice
If your business were a person, this is who they would be. This is how they would talk, think and act. Why is this even a thing? It's all about being consistent. Consistency creates familiarity and familiarity brings trust. And trust is currency. Think about that.
